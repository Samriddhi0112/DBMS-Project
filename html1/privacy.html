Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at FlexFusion Fitness

1. Introduction

Welcome to FlexFusion Fitness. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

We may collect your name, email address, fitness goals, weight data, and usage behavior to personalize your experience. Data may be collected during sign-up, workout tracking, or goal setting.

Your data helps us tailor workout plans, track your progress, offer support, and provide updates. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance website functionality and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use analytics or hosting services that have their own privacy practices. FlexFusion ensures all third parties comply with data protection standards.

6. Your Rights

You can request access to or deletion of your data at any time. Just reach out to us via the Contact Us page.

7. Security Measures

We use secure protocols to protect your information and continuously update our systems to prevent breaches.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Major changes will be notified via our homepage or through email.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out to us via our Contact Us page.
