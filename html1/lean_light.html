Full Vegetarian Plan

Welcome to the full Lean & Light Diet Plan. Below is your detailed vegetarian meal plan for the week:

Day 1

  • Breakfast: Scrambled tofu with spinach and whole-grain toast
  • Lunch: Lentil and chickpea salad with tahini dressing
  • Dinner: Grilled paneer with quinoa and roasted vegetables

Day 2

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with chia seeds, granola, and fresh fruits
  • Lunch: Vegetable stir-fry with tofu and brown rice
  • Dinner: Spinach and ricotta stuffed whole-grain tortillas

Day 3

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with almonds, raisins, and a drizzle of honey
  • Lunch: Quinoa bowl with roasted sweet potatoes and chickpeas
  • Dinner: Baked eggplant with tomato sauce and mozzarella

Day 4

  • Breakfast: Avocado toast with a sprinkle of chili flakes
  • Lunch: Spinach and feta cheese salad with balsamic dressing
  • Dinner: Mushroom risotto with a side of steamed asparagus

Day 5

  • Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with banana, spinach, almond milk, and granola
  • Lunch: Whole-grain pasta with pesto and roasted cherry tomatoes
  • Dinner: Paneer tikka with a side of mint chutney

Day 6

  • Breakfast: Protein pancakes with maple syrup and mixed berries
  • Lunch: Grilled vegetable sandwich with hummus
  • Dinner: Stuffed bell peppers with quinoa and black beans

Day 7

  • Breakfast: Overnight oats with almond butter and chia seeds
  • Lunch: Buddha bowl with tofu, avocado, and tahini dressing
  • Dinner: Veggie burger with sweet potato fries
