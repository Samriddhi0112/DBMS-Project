Full Keto Plan

Welcome to the full Keto Kickstart Diet Plan. Below is your detailed ketogenic meal plan for the week:

Day 1

  • Breakfast: Avocado and egg salad with olive oil drizzle
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken with zucchini noodles and pesto
  • Dinner: Cauliflower rice with buttered shrimp and garlic

Day 2

  • Breakfast: Keto smoothie with almond milk, spinach, and chia seeds
  • Lunch: Salmon and asparagus with lemon butter sauce
  • Dinner: Keto pizza with almond flour crust and mozzarella

Day 3

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with cheese and avocado slices
  • Lunch: Beef stir-fry with broccoli and coconut aminos
  • Dinner: Zucchini lasagna with ricotta and marinara sauce

Day 4

  • Breakfast: Keto pancakes with cream cheese and sugar-free syrup
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken Caesar salad (no croutons)
  • Dinner: Pork chops with mashed cauliflower and green beans

Day 5

  • Breakfast: Boiled eggs with avocado and smoked salmon
  • Lunch: Spinach and feta stuffed chicken breast
  • Dinner: Keto chili with sour cream and cheddar cheese

Day 6

  • Breakfast: Bulletproof coffee with grass-fed butter and MCT oil
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado lettuce wraps
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with Brussels sprouts and bacon

Day 7

  • Breakfast: Omelette with mushrooms, spinach, and feta
  • Lunch: Keto Cobb salad with ranch dressing
  • Dinner: Grilled steak with garlic butter and steamed broccoli
